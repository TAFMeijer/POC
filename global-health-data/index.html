About This Site

Built for Health Data Practitioners

This site is a personal collection of interactive tools and curated references created to make global health and health financing data more accessible.

Whether you're a researcher, policy analyst, or health system professional, these resources are designed to help you explore, query, and understand the data that shapes health policy worldwide — with a particular focus on low- and middle-income countries.
